About Us

Established in 2018, Vkonsec was founded with the mission to deliver world-class technology solutions and cater to IT projects and data center management. Our vision is to empower organizations and individuals with cutting-edge technology and practical expertise in the ever-evolving IT landscape.

Who We Are

At Vkonsec, we are a team of seasoned IT professionals with extensive expertise in networking, cybersecurity, and IT solutions. With a passion for empowering professionals and organizations, we combine our hands-on experience with innovative solutions to meet modern IT challenges.

Protect, secure, and educate — that’s our mission!

Our Services

Vkonsec offers a comprehensive range of services, focusing on both project management and training programs.

  • Specialized Training Programs

    • We provide industry-relevant training to equip learners with the skills and certifications necessary to excel in the IT industry:

    • CompTIA Network+ - CompTIA N+
    • CompTIA Security+ - CompTIA S+
    • CCNA: Cisco Certified Network Associate
    • Checkpoint: Firewall Security Solutions
    • Palo Alto: Advanced Network Security and Firewall Solutions
    • SIEM: Security Information and Event Management Systems
    • CEH: Certified Ethical Hacker
    • DevOps: Integrating Software Development and IT Operations
    • CISSP: Certified Information System Security Professional
  • Cybersecurity Consultancy & Implementation

    • As a startup focused solely on cybersecurity, Vkonsec offers end-to-end cybersecurity solutions that are vendor-neutral, ensuring world-class protection for your organization, systems, and data. Our consultancy services prioritize tailored solutions, free from technology biases or legacy issues.
